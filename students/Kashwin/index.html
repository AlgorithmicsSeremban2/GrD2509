Tools Used in Designing

Krita

A powerful tool for digital painting and illustration, especially for concept artists and illustrators. It is a great transition from GIMP for bitmap and raster graphics creation, offering more advanced brush engines and painting tools.

Canva

An easy-to-use graphic design tool with templates for creating everything from social media posts to presentations. Ideal for quick, simple design projects without the steep learning curve.

Inkscape

A free and open-source vector graphics editor, perfect for creating scalable designs and illustrations. A great alternative to Adobe Illustrator for those looking for a free tool with professional capabilities.

Blender

A comprehensive 3D modeling, animation, and rendering software used by artists and designers for animation and 3D art. It’s a popular tool for those transitioning from Tinkercad to more complex 3D design and animation projects.

Wick

A free, web-based animation and game creation tool that is great for interactive media and animations. It’s a fantastic choice for those who want to explore animation and interactive storytelling, serving as a transition from Blender for simpler animations.

Figma

A collaborative web-based design tool for UI/UX design and prototyping. Widely used in digital product design, it’s great for team collaboration and seamless design workflows.
