Essential Tools for Design & Study

Krita

Used for digital art and mind-mapping visual concepts, helping to translate complex psychological ideas into digestible visuals for patients or presentations.

Canva

Perfect for creating professional, calming infographics and resource guides for mental health awareness and patient education materials quickly and accessibly.

Inkscape

Essential for designing scalable logos and clean vector graphics for hypothetical clinic branding or creating non-distracting visual aids for studies.

Blender

Explored for creating therapeutic 3D environments or calming abstract visualizations as potential tools for guided meditation or virtual reality therapy concepts.

Wick

Used for developing simple interactive psycho-educational animations that clearly explain concepts like cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) or stress cycles.

Figma

Critical for prototyping mental health application (app) interfaces and designing user-friendly platforms focused on accessibility and a gentle user experience.
